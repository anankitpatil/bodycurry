Who Are We?

Our story is simple. Bodycurry started out as an idea... like every start up pretty much does. But the idea wasn’t to just ‘beautify’. The idea was to help people access basic skin care products like face washes, sunscreens and moisturisers, wherever and whenever without the hassle of going to a dermatologist or even to a store to buy the products. The idea was also to provide them with the basic know how of using these products in a manner that would optimize their individual skin care needs and skin health. Bodycurry is a one stop shop for all those who are looking out for basic skin care.

Since time is a premium for most of us, Skin care is often neglected, especially in the life of a working man or woman. Time constraints often make visits to a dermatologist for basic skin care recommendations not feasible. The usual tendency is to buy a product that someone suggested at random and hope that it works for you and gives you the skin that you wanted. Unfortunately, if it were that simple and people were happy with their products, why are there millions of new products introduced into the market every day? And these aren’t just any products. Top brands throw in millions into research. Why? Not because their existing products are inadequate... but because there are people who continue to be dissatisfied with those products. We intend to help you understand how to use products according to the needs of your skin. It isn’t the product that matters, it is how you use it that matters.
